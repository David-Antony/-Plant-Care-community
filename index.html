Some common questions
were often asked

My flowers are falling off or dying?

The best plants for apartments are low-maintenance ones like Snake Plants, Pothos, or Succulents that thrive in indirect light.

What causes leaves to become pale?

Yellow leaves often indicate overwatering. Ensure the top inch of soil is dry before watering again and check for proper drainage.

What causes brown crispy leaves?

Crispy edges usually mean the humidity is too low or the plant is getting too much direct sun. Try misting or moving it.

How do i choose a plant?

Consider your light levels and how much time you can dedicate to care. Beginners should start with resilient succulents.

How do I change the pots?

Gently remove the plant, loosen the roots, and place it in a pot that is 1-2 inches wider with fresh potting soil.

Why are gnats flying around my plant?

These are usually fungus gnats caused by damp soil. Let the soil dry out more between waterings to break their life cycle.
